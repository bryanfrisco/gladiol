Details

Plywood is a processed wood material made from prefabricated boards consisting of several layers of wood through a high pressure gluing and compression process. Plywood has great strength and high dimensional stability. Plywood is lightweight, easy to combine with other materials, easy to process, resistant to temperature changes, and has an aesthetic appearance.

Plywood has a wide selection of sizes and thicknesses that are more varied. Plywood is often used as a building material or furniture making material. For building materials, plywood can be used as a structural part for floors, beams, formwork (molding), and panels. Plywood can be used for building construction as paneling (room dividers, doors, windows), coating materials, floors, walls and for the construction of transportation equipment such as; aircraft (inner cladding), trains (roof, floor, walls), trucks and trailers (for bodies).

Plywood memiliki berbagai pilihan ukuran dan ketebalan yang lebih bervariasi. Plywood kerap digunakan sebagai material bahan bangunan ataupun bahan pembuat furniture.
